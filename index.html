How Stellar Lift works

No social feed, no loud gamification. Just clear numbers, soft nudges and tools that respect your attention.

One idea per app
Each tracker does one job well – drink water, count steps, log training – so you open it, update, and move on with your day.
Same language, same look
All apps follow the same layout and visual language, so learning one makes the rest feel instantly familiar.
Privacy by default
Your numbers stay on your device. No public leaderboards, no pressure to share, no “post to earn your streak”.
Realistic reminders
Gentle reminders and flexible schedules that adapt to real life – not to a perfect day that never happens.
Offline friendly
Core functionality keeps working even with spotty connection, so you can track training or water on the go.
Built by one person
Stellar Lift is created by an independent developer who actually uses these apps every day.
