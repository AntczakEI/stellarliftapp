What is Stellar Lift?

A focused ecosystem of trackers designed to remove friction and make consistency effortless.

The idea

Stellar Lift is built around a simple belief: if tracking is fast and clean, you’ll actually do it every day. Each app focuses on one job — no clutter, no distractions, no “settings maze”.

  • Single purpose apps — training, hydration, and more modules coming next.
  • Speed first — open → log → done. Minimal taps.
  • Consistency over complexity — features that help habits, not overwhelm you.
  • Privacy-aware — collect as little as possible; ads only where applicable (see Privacy Policy).
Why an ecosystem?

Most “all-in-one” apps become heavy and noisy. Stellar Lift stays light by splitting modules into separate apps — you install only what you want, and each app stays fast.
