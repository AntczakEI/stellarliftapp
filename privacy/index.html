Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Antczak Enterprise International (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our websites and mobile applications under the Stellar Lift brand (the “Services”).

What we collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. Depending on the app and features you use, we may process:

  • Technical data: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, approximate location (country/region), and diagnostic logs.
  • Usage data: screens viewed, feature interactions, and performance events (e.g., crashes).
  • App data you enter: workouts, routines, hydration entries, goals, and similar tracking data stored on your device and/or within the app’s local storage.
  • Advertising data (only in apps that show ads): the advertising ID and ad interaction events, processed by our advertising partners.

Third-party services

Some parts of our Services rely on third-party providers. These providers may collect and process technical and usage data to deliver their services.

  • Google Play (distribution, vitals, crash reporting within Play).
  • Google AdMob (only in apps that display ads).
  • Hosting/provider services used to deliver web content inside the app (for example, web app hosting). These services typically process IP address and request logs for security and reliability.

Third-party providers are responsible for their own privacy practices. Where applicable, you can review Google’s policies here: Google Privacy Policy.

How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the Services.
  • To improve performance and fix bugs (including crash diagnostics).
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To show ads and measure ad performance (only in apps that display ads).

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics/crash, ads where applicable) strictly to operate the Services.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes described above. Some diagnostic logs may be retained by third-party providers according to their policies.

Your choices

  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection by that app.
  • On Android, you can reset or limit the Advertising ID in system settings (affects apps that show ads).
  • If we use consent dialogs for ads/analytics in a specific app version, you will be able to manage your consent there.

Children

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
